The Investment Case

Morocco Is the Market.
Kénitra Is the Opportunity.

Morocco is one of the world's most accessible and legally secure destinations for international real estate investment. Foreign nationals acquire property outright under their own name, capital repatriation is supported through the formal foreign exchange framework, and the government actively courts international buyers.

Morocco's headline cities have already been discovered. Casablanca commands premium prices. Marrakech is saturated with international competition. Tangier is attracting institutional capital at scale. Kénitra is different: a rapidly transforming coastal city where the fundamentals are firmly in place and the pricing still reflects what it was, not what it is becoming.

Rahim International focuses on Kénitra because this is where the gap between current value and future trajectory is greatest. Industrial expansion, high-speed rail, Atlantic coastline, and strong rental demand make the case. The window remains open.

The Foundation

Why Morocco Stands Apart
for International Capital

Before the city, the country. Morocco provides the legal protections, economic stability, and investor framework that make Kénitra's opportunity accessible and transparent for international buyers.

01

Foreign Ownership

Foreign nationals can acquire residential property in their own name. Freehold title is notarised and registered, providing a clear ownership path for international buyers.

02

Capital Repatriation

When structured correctly through the formal foreign exchange system, qualifying purchases can support repatriation of rental income and sale proceeds.

03

Stability and Growth

Morocco combines macroeconomic stability with long-term infrastructure investment, making it one of Africa's most credible markets for capital preservation.

04

World Cup 2030

Morocco co-hosts the 2030 FIFA World Cup with Spain and Portugal, accelerating infrastructure upgrades and global attention across key urban corridors.

05

Atlantic Gateway

Morocco sits between Europe, Africa, and the Gulf, and Kénitra is embedded in the Atlantic logistics and manufacturing corridor.

06

Early Pricing

Premium residential property in Kénitra remains materially below comparable coastal and capital-adjacent markets, while the drivers of appreciation are already visible.

Coastal Living & Natural Assets

Atlantic Coastline.
National Parks. Ancient Heritage.

The investment case for Kénitra property is grounded in industrial and infrastructural fundamentals, but the lifestyle proposition is Atlantic. Five minutes from the city centre, Mehdiya's beach extends along one of Morocco's most unspoiled coastlines. The Sidi Boughaba National Park borders the shore. Kénitra offers what almost no emerging investment city can: natural assets directly adjacent to a high-growth industrial and economic hub.
