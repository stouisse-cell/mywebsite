Who We Are

Moroccan Real Estate,
Made Clearer.

Moroccan real estate still suffers from a serious information gap. Many strong local developers have limited marketing, weak international reach, no proper website, and traditional sales processes that make projects difficult to discover, compare, and understand from a distance.

For buyers outside Morocco, Moroccan diaspora families, and even local buyers investing outside their own city, reliable information is often scattered or informal. Rahim International exists to organize that process: we research the market, assess developers, compare opportunities, ask the questions buyers need answered, and help serious projects reach the right audience.

"We turn scattered information into a clear property decision."

Ali Rahim · Founder & CEO

Our Team

A Small Team,
Built Around Accountability.

Rahim International is a small team of five, built around a practical idea: buyers need professional representation close to the market, and developers need credible access to buyers beyond their local network.

Our core team is based in Kénitra, supported by two agents abroad: one in Canada for North American clients, and one in France for European clients. That gives buyers a regional point of contact while keeping the real market work anchored in Morocco.

Our Background

Why the Bridge
Matters

Our logo is inspired by the Mohammed VI Bridge, an architectural and engineering landmark that reflects Morocco's rapid development over the past fifteen years: modern infrastructure, national ambition, and a country building toward its future.

Our home city, Kénitra, means "little bridge." That connection is not accidental. Rahim International exists to be a bridge of its own: between Moroccan real estate and the buyers who cannot always be here to see it for themselves.

For Buyers Abroad
We protect your time, your money, and your confidence by doing the groundwork before you commit.

For Moroccan diaspora buyers, that means someone local who can inspect, compare, verify, and follow up when you are back in Europe, North America, or the Gulf. For international investors, it means making Morocco understandable: ownership, banking, notaries, neighbourhoods, rental potential, and developer quality.

For developers, our role is different but connected. We are not just a sales channel. We combine business analysis, development strategy, marketing, and buyer distribution to turn good local projects into credible investment opportunities.

For Developers
We help shape the investment case, sharpen the positioning, and bring the project to buyers beyond the local market.

"Distance is the risk. Reliable local guidance is the difference."
