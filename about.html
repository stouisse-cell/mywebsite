Our Background

Experience Earned,
Not Theoretical

Rahim International is based in Kénitra — a city whose name means "little bridge" in Arabic. It is not a coincidence. Our logo is a bridge. Our business is a bridge. And the city we call home has been a bridge between Morocco's Atlantic coast and its capital for generations.

Kénitra is a city on the rise — home to Morocco's newest TGV station, a growing industrial and technology corridor, and a real estate market that has not yet been discovered by international buyers. We intend to change that.

Our Mandate Approach
We do not take listings. We take mandates — deploying our complete capability set with full commitment to every project we partner with.

We serve two distinct audiences. For property developers, we act as a fully integrated international sales and marketing partner — from investment case architecture through to transaction close. For international buyers, we are a trusted guide from first enquiry through to key handover.

Based in Kénitra and operating across Europe, North America, and the Gulf, we bring a global perspective to every mandate — backed by rigorous academic foundations in International Business Administration, International Marketing, and Global Supply Chain Management.
