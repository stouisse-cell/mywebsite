About

A Different
Kind of Firm

Rahim International is a cross-border real estate advisory firm headquartered in Kénitra, specialising in Kénitra property and the wider Moroccan market. We serve two audiences: developers who need international reach, and buyers — whether Moroccan diaspora or foreign nationals — who need a trusted guide through the entire acquisition process.

We are not a listing platform. We take mandates, deploy a complete capability set — strategic advisory, premium marketing, international distribution — and deliver results. For buyers, we manage every step from property selection to key handover.

"Kénitra is one of the most compelling property markets in Morocco right now — and Morocco is one of the most compelling markets in the world. The problem has never been the product — it has been the bridge to the right buyers. That is what we are."

Ali Rahim — Founder & CEO
