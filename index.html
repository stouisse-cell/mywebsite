About

A Different
Kind of Firm

Rahim International is a cross-border real estate advisory firm headquartered in Kénitra, Morocco. We serve two audiences with equal depth: developers who need credible international reach, and buyers, whether Moroccan diaspora or foreign nationals, who need a trusted, end-to-end guide through the Moroccan acquisition process.

We are not a listing platform. We take mandates, deploy a complete capability set, and deliver results. For buyers, we manage every step from property selection through to key handover, in the language and timezone that suits you.

"Kénitra is one of the most compelling emerging property markets in Morocco, and Morocco is one of the most compelling markets in the world. The problem has never been the product. It has been the bridge to the right buyers. That is what we are."

Ali Rahim · Founder & CEO
